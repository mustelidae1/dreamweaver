Welcome to my portfolio! I believe that media hold a special place in our hearts, and that the lifeblood of a civilization is its stories. And in a world as divided and isolated as the one that we live in, I think we need positive media more than ever before. As an interactive developer, it is my goal to create media that educate, entertain and inspire. I am an award-winning game designer and currently attending Boise State University with a double major in GIMM (Games, Interactive Media and Mobile Development) and Computer Science. I have a particular interest in working with cutting edge technologies like AR and VR, as well as interactive music.
